        1 - 1D Kinematics

        Content

        • Topics and Readings

          Topics:  Motion diagrams, graphs, problem solving strategy, the particle model, position and displacement, velocity, acceleration, free fall, and inclined motion.

          Reading:  Chapters 1 and 2 from the textbook.  Give special attention to section 1.8 in the textbook, as we won't be covering it in detail during lecture.

          Suggested Videos: 

          • Acceleration
          • Graphical Analysis
          • Speed and Velocity

          Suggested examples:   2.4, 2.10, 2.11, 2.12, and 2.13

          Suggested end-of-chapter problems:  Chapter 2, problems 13, 15, 17, 19, 21, 27, 29, 33, 35, 49, 51, 53, 57.
